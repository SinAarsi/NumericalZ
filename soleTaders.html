Sole Trader - Bookkeeping, Accounts and Tax Returns


If you are trading as a sole trader and don't want the hassle of trading as a limited company, we can help you make things easier.

We use cloud accounting software such as Quickbooks so that you have access to your bookkeeping no matter where you are. We provide you with training and use of the program so you are always aware of your current business performance at the click of a button.

We offer Following Services to our clients:
  • Bookkeeping on cloud accounting software such as Quickbooks.
  • VAT Returns preparation and submission.
  • Accounts and tax return preparation.
  • Submission of tax returns to the HMRC.
  • VAT Registration (if turnover exceeds the VAT treshhold).
  • Self assessment registration with HMRC (if required).
