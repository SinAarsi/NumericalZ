At NumaricalZ, we understand the unique challenges faced by subcontractors in the UK construction industry. Whether you're a tradesperson, a construction worker, or a specialist contractor, managing your subcontracting business can be complex. That's where we come in.


Our Serices

We offer a comprehensive range of services tailored to meet the needs of subcontractors:


CIS Registration and Compliance: We'll help you navigate the Construction Industry Scheme (CIS) registration process and ensure that you remain compliant with CIS regulations, including verifying subcontractors and submitting CIS returns.
Tax Planning and Advice: Our team of experienced tax professionals will provide personalized tax planning and advice to help you minimize your tax liabilities and maximize your tax savings.
Bookkeeping and Accounting: Stay on top of your finances with our bookkeeping and accounting services. From invoicing and expense tracking to financial reporting and payroll processing, we'll handle the financial side of your business so you can focus on what you do best.
Contract Review and Negotiation: We'll review your contracts and help you negotiate favorable terms with main contractors, ensuring that you're protected and fairly compensated for your work.


Why Choose Us ?


Expertise: Our team consists of qualified accountants and industry experts with extensive experience in serving subcontractors in the construction industry.
Personalized Service: We take the time to understand your unique business needs and objectives and tailor our services to meet your specific requirements.
Compliance: With our in-depth knowledge of CIS regulations and tax laws, you can trust that your business is in compliance with all applicable regulations and requirements.
Peace of Mind: Focus on growing your business with confidence, knowing that your financial affairs are in capable hands.
